# Planets Quick Notes

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
Venus has a thick atmosphere and extreme surface temperatures.
Earth has liquid water oceans and one natural moon.
Mars has Olympus Mons, one of the largest volcanoes in the solar system.
Jupiter is a gas giant with a long-lived Great Red Spot storm.
Saturn is known for its ring system.
Uranus rotates on a very tilted axis.
Neptune has strong winds and appears deep blue.
