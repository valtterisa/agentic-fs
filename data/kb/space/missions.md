# Space Missions Snapshot

Apollo 11 landed humans on the Moon in 1969.
Voyager 1 and Voyager 2 explored the outer planets and continue in interstellar space.
The Hubble Space Telescope has provided deep-space observations for decades.
The James Webb Space Telescope observes in infrared and studies early galaxies.
Perseverance is a Mars rover investigating ancient habitability and collecting samples.
